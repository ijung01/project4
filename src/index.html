Let us take you there!

A fleet of 24 free shuttles that daily travel four routes in the central business district of Baltimore City, Maryland. We service residents, downtown employees, students and tourists and anyone else who wishes to ride. The shuttle intends to reduce congestion and greenhouse gas pollution by offering a convenient, reliable and eco-friendly form of public transportation. index.html
